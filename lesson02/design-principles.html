Hick's Law

DoorDash

DoorDash

DoorDash website and app has a lot of pictures from the dishes, so many food options, also Covenience stores, Groceries and so many more, this makes the user take longer to find what he or she wants thus taking more time to make a decision. On DoorDash app and website you can filter the option with many entries like popularity, regional foods and customer ratings, making easier to customer make a decision.
